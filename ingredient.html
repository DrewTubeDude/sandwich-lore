What ingredients can you put in a sandwich?

As long as you are brave enough, you can put nearly anything on a sandwich!
Whether it be bacon, anchovies, or the almighty CHEESE SLICE, anything goes!
One of my personal favorites is yellow mustard, due to its versatility, flavor,
and characteristic color! Of course, we can't forget the all important bread, the most crucial
medium for our other ingredients to lie between. Some may argue that a hot dog is a sandwich,
but that is clearly nonsense, because it is most certainly a burrito. Tune in next week to find
out once and for all if a soup is in fact a salad!
