What makes a sandwich good?

To decide what makes a sandwich good, we must first determine what our parameters for 'good' are.
I believe the ideal sandwich to have three core properties: Structure, flavor, and texture.
Without structure, eating the sandwich becomes a hassle. No flavor? Why even eat a sandwich at
that point. No texture? Easily the worst of the three by my standards, since despising the feel
of food even being in your mouth sounds horrible. At least if its bland or falls apart I can
still eat it right? Thus, conclusively, I believe the ideal sandwich to be robust from crust to crust,
delicious beyond a reasonable doubt, and should crunch nicely when eaten.
